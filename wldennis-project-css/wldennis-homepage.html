About this Site

This site is a passion project where I detail some of my favorite hip-hop music. I want to share my love of music with other people, and hopefully get people to listen to some of the music that I enjoy. Hip-hop music is something that I am very passionate about and I want to spread awareness of some lesser known songs and albums.

About Me

My name is Wyatt Dennis and I am a sophomore at Syracuse University in New York. I am studying television/radio/film at the Newhouse School of Public Communications and information management and technology at the iSchool. I am from Danville, California, which is about a 40 minute drive from San Francisco. My interests include music, film, videogames, and having a good time with friends. In the future I would like to work in the film industry, the music industry, or in the IT business. I have a huge passion in hip-hop music, and I even produce my own beats.
